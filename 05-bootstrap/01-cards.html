Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released by Google in 2014. It is simple, but it became famous because many people play it when their internet connection stops working.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game was released in 2019 by House House. Players solve puzzles by causing trouble as a goose in a quiet village.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird was released in 2013 by Dong Nguyen. It became popular because it was very simple to play but very hard to master.
